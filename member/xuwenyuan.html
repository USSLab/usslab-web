Research Interests


My research interests are wireless networking, and its security and privacy. I have a particular interest in building reliable wireless networks and securing commodity wireless devices.

• Wireless Network Security and privacy

• Jamming/Radio Interference

• Cyber Security

• Industry Control System Security

Apply to participate in my group

Apply to do independent study or be a member of the research team in my group, please drop me an email. I am happy to work with both undergraduate and graduate students. Postdocs may also be available.
